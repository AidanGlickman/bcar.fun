What does the 'r' mean?

The 'r' in BCAr stands for redirect. Also BCA.fun was not available.

Why .fun?

What could be more fun than school? (and it was cheap.)

BCAr.fun is a quick project I started on in early 2018 by just having a bunch of subdomain redirects so that I could more easily remember the domain names for all of BCA's websites. As time went on, and more people asked me about BCAr.fun, I decided that I would make a quick landing page so that people could easily find all of the various BCA websites super quickly.
